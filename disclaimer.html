Price Data & Accuracy

Spot price data is sourced from third-party market data APIs and is updated periodically throughout the trading day. While we strive for accuracy, prices displayed on this site may lag real-time market conditions by several minutes. During periods of high market volatility, discrepancies may be larger.

The melt value calculated by our tools represents the intrinsic silver content value at the displayed spot price. The actual amount you receive when selling will always differ from melt value due to:

  • Dealer spreads — Buyers purchase below spot to cover their own costs and profit margin. Typical offers range from 50% to 98% of melt value depending on the buyer type and quantity.
  • Refining and processing fees — Refiners deduct costs for melting, assaying, and processing your silver before settlement.
  • Assay uncertainty — Hallmarks indicate intended purity; actual silver content may vary slightly due to manufacturing tolerances.
  • Condition and form — Damaged, plated, or mixed-content items may yield less than the calculated melt value.
