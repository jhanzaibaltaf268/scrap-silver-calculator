Silver Bar Sizes

Silver bars come in standardized sizes, from small 1-ounce bars to large 100-ounce industrial bars. The larger the bar, the lower the premium over spot price.

  • 1 oz bars — Popular for small investors, easy to trade
  • 10 oz bars — Most popular size for individual investors
  • 100 oz bars — Lower premiums, preferred by serious investors
  • 1 kg bars — International standard, common outside the US
