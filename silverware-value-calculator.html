⚠️ Silver-Plated vs Solid Silver

Silver-plated items (marked "EPNS", "EP", or "Silver Plate") have only a thin coating of silver and minimal melt value. This calculator is for solid silver items marked "925", "STERLING", "800", etc.

How to Value Silverware

The value of silverware depends on whether it's solid silver or silver-plated:

  • Solid silver (925, 800, etc.) — Worth its weight in silver
  • Silver-plated — Worth very little for silver content

Selling Silverware Tips

  • Complete silverware sets in good condition may be worth more than melt value
  • Separate solid silver from silver-plated pieces before selling
