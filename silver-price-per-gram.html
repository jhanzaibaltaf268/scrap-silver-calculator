How Much Is a Gram of Silver Worth?

Silver is globally priced in troy ounces (31.1035g) but most people weigh items in grams. The per-gram price is simply the troy ounce spot price divided by 31.1035. How to Calculate Silver Price Per Gram

  1. Find your silver's purity stamp — look for 925, 999, 800, 900, or similar markings stamped on the item (clasp, back of handle, base).
  2. Weigh it in grams — use a digital scale accurate to 0.01g. Postal scales and kitchen scales are often accurate enough for large items.
  3. Apply the formula — (weight in grams ÷ 31.1035) × purity × spot price = melt value

Example: 25g of sterling silver at today's spot of $—:
