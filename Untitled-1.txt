## Refining Made Better
#### Fastest payments
Elemetal offers rapid, same-day payment for scrap gold, silver and bullion products. Most lots are processed in 1-2 hours or less, prioritizing your business's cash flow, whether you walk-in or ship.

#### Diverse settlement
Get paid today through a wide variety of settlement options including wire, ACH / direct deposit, bullion trade, or metal exchange.

#### Secure lock ins
Hedge against market fluctuations with Elemetal's secure lock in pricing! Commercial customers can call up to five business days in advance to lock in current precious-metal spot pricing.

#### Express shipping
Elemetal provides safe and convenient shipping using our Express Shipping Program. Know that your packages are insured and quickly transported. To join our Express Shipping Program, call 469-522-1111.

#### Daily bullion pricing
Our daily trade pricing sheet shows you the lowest premiums on bullion products from Elemetal. To join the mailing list, just email [[email protected]](https://www.elemetal.com/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection)

We offer gemstone removal, process bench sweeps, and pay on a vast range of jewelry scrap. Elemetal offers the fastest payments and in-person service to help ensure you maximize your precious metal return.
