What Is a Scrap Silver Calculator?

A scrap silver calculator determines the intrinsic melt value of a silver item — not its retail price or sentimental value. It answers: if you melted this item down to pure silver today, what would that metal be worth? Our engine utilizes real-time market data to provide the same valuation used by professional refineries.

Melt Value = (Weight × Purity) ÷ 31.1035 × Spot Price

How Silver Melt Value Is Calculated

The formula depends on three factors: weight, purity, and the current spot price. Silver is internationaly priced in troy ounces (31.1035 grams). Our calculator handles all conversions between grams, ounces, and kilograms automatically.

Understanding Silver Purity Marks

Look for hallmarks like 925 (Sterling), 999 (Fine Silver), or 900 (Coin Silver). These tell you the percentage of silver in the alloy. Real silver is never magnetic and often carries these stamps.
