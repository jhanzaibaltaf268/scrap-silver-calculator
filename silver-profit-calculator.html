How to Track Your Silver Investments

The Silver Profit Calculator is designed for stackers, investors, and scrappers to quickly verify exactly how much their portfolio has grown. It compares your average purchase price against today's live silver spot price.

Understanding Dollar Cost Averaging

If you buy silver multiple times over a year, your Average Purchase Price is what matters. To calculate it, divide your total money spent by your total ounces acquired. Entering this average into the calculator will give you a perfect snapshot of your entire stack's profitability.

Premiums vs. Spot Price

Remember that when you buy bullion (like American Silver Eagles), you pay a "premium" above the spot price. To get an accurate ROI on this calculator, enter your total cost per ounce including that premium into the "Purchase Price" field, rather than what the spot price was on the day you bought it.
