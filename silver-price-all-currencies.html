Why Silver Prices Differ by Currency

Silver is priced globally in US dollars per troy ounce. When you convert to other currencies, the exchange rate determines the final price. This means silver can effectively go "up" in one currency while going "down" in another, depending on exchange rate movements.

Understanding Silver in South Asian Markets

In India and Pakistan, silver is commonly priced per tola (11.6638 grams). Our converter automatically shows prices in local formats.

Silver Price Factors

  • USD spot price — The global base price
  • Exchange rates — Fluctuate based on economic conditions
  • Local premiums — Physical silver may carry additional premiums in different markets
  • Import duties — Some countries add duties, particularly India and China
