What Is Gold Spot Price?

The spot price is the current market price for one troy ounce of 99.9% pure (24K) gold. This price is updated continuously during market hours and serves as the baseline for calculating the value of any gold item by purity.

All gold prices above are calculated from the current spot price multiplied by the purity percentage and converted to the desired unit of weight.
