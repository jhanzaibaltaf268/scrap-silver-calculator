Reporting a Price or Calculation Error

Silver spot prices are sourced from live market APIs and update regularly throughout the trading day. If you notice a significant discrepancy between the price shown here and major market sources, please use the form to report it with the time and price you observed. We investigate all reports within one business day.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer an API for silver prices?
We don't currently offer a public API, but if there's demand we'll consider it. Drop us a message if this is something you'd use.

Can I embed your calculator on my website?
We don't currently offer an embeddable widget, but it's on our roadmap. Contact us with details about your use case.

How do I report a hallmark or purity standard that's missing?
Use the contact form and describe the hallmark, its country of origin, and the silver percentage. We'll add it to the purity chart and calculator if we can verify it.

Is the site free to use?
Yes — all calculators, tools, and price data are completely free. The site is supported by advertising and may include affiliate links. See our Disclaimer for full details.
