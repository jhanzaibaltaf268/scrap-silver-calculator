What Is 10K Gold?

10K gold is 41.7% pure gold — the minimum purity standard for items to be legally called "gold" in the USA. It contains 58.3% other metals (copper, nickel, etc.), making it extremely durable and affordable. Common in budget jewelry, wedding rings, and everyday pieces.

Where You'll Find 10K Gold

  • Budget wedding rings and engagement rings
  • Affordable fine jewelry
  • Class rings (high school, college)
  • Watch cases
  • Costume jewelry that's marked as real gold

10K Gold Value

Despite lower gold content, 10K gold has significant scrap value. Most dealers pay 75–90% of melt value. Use this calculator to find your item's exact worth.
