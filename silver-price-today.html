Market Context

What Drives Silver Prices Every Day

The major forces moving XAG/USD. Click below to see all 9 factors.

Industrial Demand

Solar panels, electronics, EVs, water purification. ~55% of annual demand is industrial. Strong manufacturing lifts prices; recessions drag them.

US Dollar Strength

Silver moves inversely to USD. Strong dollar = expensive silver for foreign buyers (lower demand). Dollar weakness boosts international flows.

Inflation Expectations

Silver is an inflation hedge. Surprise CPI prints or loose policy signals lift investment demand sharply as investors seek tangible value.

Investment Flows & ETFs

Silver ETF holdings, futures positioning, and retail accumulation drive price action. Risk-on sentiment sends money into silver fast.

Mine Supply Disruptions

Top producers: Mexico, Peru, Russia, China. Strikes or shutdowns tighten supply overnight. A single major closure can move prices 5–10%.

Interest Rates & Central Banks

Silver pays no yield, so rising rates reduce its appeal vs. bonds. Fed pivots to cuts or pauses trigger sharp precious metals rallies.

Gold/Silver Ratio

Gold ÷ silver typically ranges 60–90. Above 80 = silver undervalued. Today's ratio: —. Value investors use this for timing.

Geopolitical Events

Wars, sanctions, and trade tensions trigger risk-off flows into precious metals. Peace signals reverse these moves within days.

Economic Data Releases

US jobs, CPI, GDP reports signal future rate direction. Strong employment pushes silver down; weak data lifts it on rate-cut hopes.

What Is the Silver Spot Price Today?

Today's silver spot price is $— per troy ounce — the global wholesale reference rate on COMEX and the London Bullion Market. Updated hourly. The universal benchmark used by dealers, refiners, investors, and central banks.

Spot vs. What You Actually Receive

Spot is the rate for immediate delivery of 999 silver with no premiums. Buying: you pay spot + a dealer premium (5–20% for bars, more for coins). Selling scrap: you receive spot minus the dealer's discount (typically 5–40%). Comparing 3+ dealers can earn you 20–30% more per ounce.
