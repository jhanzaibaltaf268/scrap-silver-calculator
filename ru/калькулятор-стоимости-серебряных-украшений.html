How to Value Silver Jewelry

The melt value of silver jewelry is based on three factors: weight, purity, and the current spot price. Most silver jewelry is 925 sterling silver (92.5% pure).

Tips for Weighing Silver Jewelry

  • Use a digital scale accurate to 0.1 grams
  • Remove any gemstones or non-silver components before weighing
  • Check for hallmarks to identify the purity
  • Designer pieces may be worth more than melt value
