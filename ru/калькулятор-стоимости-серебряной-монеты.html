How to Calculate Silver Coin Value

To find a silver coin's melt value, you need to know its total weight and silver purity. Most collectible and circulating silver coins are 90% (US coins) or 80% (Canadian coins) silver.

  • Weigh the coin on a precise scale
  • Look up the coin's silver content percentage
  • Our calculator multiplies weight × purity × spot price

For pre-1965 US coins, try our dedicated Junk Silver Calculator which has preset values for all common US silver coins.
