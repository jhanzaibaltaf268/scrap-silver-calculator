99.9% Pure — The Highest Purity Silver

999 fine silver is the highest standard of pure silver, containing 99.9% silver content with only 0.1% of other metals. This makes it the most valuable form of investment-grade silver and the preferred choice for collectors and bullion investors.

How to Identify 999 Silver

Check the Hallmark

  • → Look for "999" or ".999" stamp
  • → Should be clearly visible on item
  • → Often accompanied by maker's mark

Test the Weight

  • → Use precise scale (±0.1g accuracy)
  • → Pure silver is denser than most metals
  • → Verify against known samples

Visual Inspection

  • → Bright, lustrous finish
  • → No discoloration or dark spots
  • → High conductivity (magnetic test)
