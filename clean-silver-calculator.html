What is Scrap Silver?

Scrap silver refers to any silver item that is valued purely for its metal content rather than its artistic or historic significance. This includes broken jewelry, old flatware, and common-dated coins. Melt value is the standard baseline used by refineries to determine the cash value of these items.

How is Melt Value Calculated?

Industry professionals use a standard formula to calculate intrinsic value:

  • Melt Value = (Weight × Purity) × Spot Price

Reading Hallmarks

Fineness markings are essential for valuation. Always check your items for the following:

  • 999 — Investment grade fine silver.
  • 925 — Sterling silver, the global jewelry standard.
  • 900 — Traditional US coinage silver.
  • 800/835 — Common European flatware standards.
