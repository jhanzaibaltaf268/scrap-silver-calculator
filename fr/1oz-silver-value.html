How Much Is 1 Ounce of Silver Worth?

The 1 troy ounce silver price is identical to the global spot price. One troy ounce (31.1035 grams) of .999 fine silver is the base unit for silver pricing worldwide.

1oz silver bars and coins are the most popular investment size due to their affordability and easy divisibility.
