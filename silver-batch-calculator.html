How to Use the Batch Calculator

The Batch Calculator lets you add multiple silver items and calculate their combined value — ideal when you're preparing to sell a collection to a dealer.

  1. Add items — Click "+ Add Another Item" for each piece
  2. Enter details — Description, weight, unit, and purity for each
  3. See total — The grand total updates automatically
  4. Print or copy — Take the receipt to your dealer or save it for reference
💡 Pro Tip

Group items by purity (all 925 pieces together, all 800 pieces together) for easier negotiation with dealers. Use the "Save to Browser" button to keep your inventory between visits.
